Welcome

Welcome to the Katwijk community website !
If you are a current resident of Katwijk, please feel free to register and become part of our online community.
  • This site has been created to bring the residents of Katwijk together and enable all of us to embrace our sense of community. Whether you are the "new kid on the block" or the resident that knows all there is about Katwijk, being part of this online community will enrich your experience of living here. In Katwijk we share. Now you no longer have to miss out on the generous offerings in your community.
  • The site enables community members to advertside their offerings under categories such as bicycles, garden, invitations, home, household, meals, pet related, produce and other.
  • As a member of the Katwijk community you are always welcome to contact the site administrator should you have questions, suggestions, concerns or require help using the site. See our contact page for details.
  • Never waste another oliebol or let an old bicycle take up space in your shed. Register and share in the joy of giving !
  • When members give their consent, we are also sharing images and information of past offerings on our facebook, instagram and twitter accounts.
  • To register as a member you need to create your username, provide your firstname, surname, postal code, create your own password and confirm that you agree to abide by the rules as set out on the registration page.
    • Once you have created your online profile, you will be able to see all the offers as posted by our online community members and be able to post your own.
